§ 02 / the three self-sabotages

You know exactly how you blow up. You do it anyway.

01

Moving the stop.

Price runs against you, you widen the SL "just this once." One trade later it doesn't come back. The single most destructive habit in retail trading — and the hardest to stop manually.

→ Cause of 40%+ of retail account blowups
02

Revenge trades.

A loss stings, so you size up on the next entry to "get it back." Your frontal cortex isn't driving the next order — the amygdala is. Statistics don't care.

→ ~40% of traders blow up in the session after a loss
03

No hard limits.

Stops, alerts, journals, coaches — all advisory. The moment you're tilted, the advice is the first thing that goes. You need a system that cancels orders, not one that asks nicely.

→ Advisory tools have 0% enforcement

§ 04 / how it works

Up and running in 60 seconds.

STEP 01

Connect to RiskGuard bot.

Open Telegram, message @RiskGuardBot, tap /connect. A one-time secure webview walks you through pairing your Hyperliquid account via a scoped agent key — cancel and reduce-only permission, no withdrawal rights.

⏱ ~30 seconds
STEP 02

Set your rules.

Pick your daily loss cap, profit lock, cooldown window, and which rules to enforce. Defaults: 5% / 100% / 8 AM EDT / all on. Change them any time by messaging /rules.

⏱ ~20 seconds
STEP 03

Trade protected.

Place orders on Hyperliquid exactly as you do today — web, mobile, TradingView, whatever. The bot polls your account every 3 seconds and cancels any order that violates your rules, before it can damage you.

⏱ zero workflow change
STEP 04

Monitor on Telegram.

Every cancel, every lock, every restored stop — pushed straight to your chat. Ask the bot /status for live P&L, /log for today's actions, /pause to disable temporarily. Your dashboard lives in a thread.

⏱ always-on
